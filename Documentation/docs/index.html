What the platform does

TherapyConnect is a back-office system for a solo (or small) therapy practice. The therapist is the primary authenticated actor. Through a React single-page app they can:

  • Manage clients — create client records, update details, mark active/terminated, flag DSF (discounted/sliding-fee) clients.
  • Define services & delivery modes — e.g. "Individual Therapy" delivered online (Google Meet) or offline (in-person at an address), each with its own price.
  • Publish availability — weekly recurring rules plus date-specific overrides are expanded into bookable time slots.
  • Book & manage appointments — schedule, confirm, reschedule, complete, cancel or abandon sessions against available slots.
  • Auto-sync calendars — confirmed appointments create Google Calendar invites (with Meet links for online sessions) for therapist and client.
  • Track the business — dashboards for earnings, completion/cancellation rates, service breakdowns and client retention.
  • Encrypted session notes — clinical notes are stored AES-GCM encrypted at the field level.
